What is Neonatal Nursing?

Neonatal nursing is a subspecialty of nursing that works with newborn infants with a variety of problems. Problems like birth defects,infections,cardiac malformation,surgical problems,and being born prematurely.Neonatal nursing encompasses care for infants who experience long-term problems right after birth, but they also care for infants who experience long-term problems.

How much they make?

Neonatal nurses make an avegrage annual salary of $94,480.In Connecticut they make an average of $101,840.Overall a Neonatal nurse salary depends on several factors like experience,workplace,education level,and location.
